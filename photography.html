Photography

Because I stare at screens for most of the working day I've been rediscovering my love for all-analog photography -- shooting on film, developing at home, and printing on photographic paper. This is a little diary of some favorite prints that I've made along the way.

I apologize for the potato quality on some of these. I "scan" the finished product just using my camera phone without any fancy color correction or enhancement aside from some quick twiddling of knobs on Google Photos.

If you're inspired by these and want to chat, you can find my contact on my resume. I'm also on Instagram at @yichiphoto.
